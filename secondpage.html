Red vs White

Red meat is meat that is red! Raw meat may contain harmful bacteria including Salmonella, Listeria, Campylobacter and E. coli that can cause food poisoning. These bacteria are destroyed when meat is correctly cooked. Red meat includes beef, mutton, and goat.

White Meat

White meat is meat that is white! Raw poultry can vary from a bluish-white to yellow. All of these colors are normal and are a direct result of breed, exercise, age, and/or diet. Younger poultry has less fat under the skin, which can cause the bluish cast, and the yellow skin could be a result of marigolds in the feed. White meats include chicken, turkey, and rabbit.
